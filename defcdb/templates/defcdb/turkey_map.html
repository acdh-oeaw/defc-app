MAP OF REGIONS AND DISTRICTS IN TURKEY

This work, "Archaeological regions of Turkey" is a derivative of the "Turkey location map" by NordNordWest.
The present map of regions and districts in Turkey was created by the AAPP research group. The division of regions is therefore a research tool that has also been employed by the DEFC app. The districts correspond to the official districts of Turkey.
